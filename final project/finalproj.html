WELCOME

to the world of california Garden!

In 1980, California Garden Brand was born from a
simple idea, to supply the Far East and the Middle
East with quality food products from California.

Since our founding, we have followed a simple philosophy: to provide
the consumer with quality products at reasonable prices.

Our strength is innovation, both in manufacturing and packaging.
Today we offer over two hundred products processed in over twenty
Countries around the World as we celebrate our 40th year of innovation.
We are proud of our products and we love what we do!
